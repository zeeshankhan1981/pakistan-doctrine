Our Mission

Pakistan Under Siege is dedicated to archiving and exposing human rights violations perpetrated by the military and unelected government since 2022. Our mission is to provide a transparent, accessible record for the world and empower those seeking justice.

Warning: Contributors and sources are at risk of harassment, abduction, or worse. All information is published at great personal risk. This site is not affiliated with any political party or foreign agency. Our only allegiance is to the truth.

Documentation Process

  • Collecting verified reports from reputable sources
  • Organizing incidents by region, date, and type
  • Ensuring transparency and accuracy
  • Making data accessible for advocacy and research
