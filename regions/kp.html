Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KP)

KP endures drone strikes, collective punishment, and the systematic repression of the Pashtun Tahafuz Movement. Arbitrary detentions and torture are common. The region is in a permanent state of emergency, with its people caught between militants and the military. The struggle for dignity and rights is ongoing.
