Balochistan: Under Siege

Balochistan remains the epicenter of enforced disappearances, extrajudicial killings, and military operations in Pakistan. In 2024, over 150 cases of enforced disappearances and dozens of extrajudicial killings were reported, with countless families still searching for answers. (HRCP)

Recent Incidents (2023–2024)

Enforced Disappearances in Turbat

On 15 February 2023, multiple cases of enforced disappearances were reported in Turbat district. At least 12 individuals went missing after being detained by security forces. HRCP, HRW

Extrajudicial Killings in Gwadar

On 20 April 2023, three civilians were killed in a police operation in Gwadar. Eyewitnesses reported a lack of proper investigation. Amnesty International

Survivor Testimony

“My brother was taken from our home in Turbat. We have not heard from him in over a year. Every day, my mother waits for news. We just want him back.”
— Testimony collected by HRCP, 2023
